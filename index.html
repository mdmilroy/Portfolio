WHO I AM

Hi, my name is Michael Milroy. I was born and raised in Indiana, focused on computers. I carried that interest over to a successful career in the Marine Corps as a Signals Intelligence Operator and Analyst. I provided geolocation support and lead multiple teams of other intelligence operators from multiple military branches.

I finished my Bachelor's degree at Purdue University while raising a family and studying full-time. I then began working in project management for the State of Indiana. I helped lead an agile transformation for the Bureau of Motor Vehicles and have since been working in an Agile Coach role. I work on an agile team of process improvement specialists guiding the agency to success.

I have worked very hard to get where I am today. I love learning and using my knowledge, skills, and abilities to help others succeed wherever possible. I am a team player that loves living in the details, but can still maintain a solid understanding of the big picture. I enjoy learning different programming languages and seeing how they can interact to build an even better product. I am taking all of my experience and working to become a successful developer.
